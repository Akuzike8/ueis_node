About UEIS
About the System

Eletronic Identification System UEIS (UEIS) is a system that enables individuals to use a single electronic identity to authenticate themselves across various digital services. Benefits of using UEIS includes increased security, convenience, and cost-effectiveness. The system also poses significant challenges, including privacy concerns, data protection, and cybersecurity risks which might not be directly linked to our delivery model. Overall, the UEIS has significant implications on individuals, governments, and society as a whole.
